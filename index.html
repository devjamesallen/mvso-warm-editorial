Our Purpose

Entertain. Enrich.
Inspire.

The Miami Valley Symphony Orchestra brings together accomplished musicians from across the region to deliver performances that move, challenge, and inspire. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, every note is driven by passion — not profit.

Our mission is to advance the knowledge and appreciation of orchestral music, making it accessible and alive for everyone in the Miami Valley.

Who We Are

The Miami Valley Symphony Orchestra

Dedicated to advancing orchestral music through passionate performance and community engagement.

Our Mission

Founded in 1990, the Miami Valley Symphony Orchestra has established itself as a beacon of classical music in Ohio. We are driven by a simple but profound mission: to advance the knowledge and appreciation of orchestral music through passionate performance and meaningful community engagement.

With seventy accomplished volunteer musicians, we deliver world-class performances that celebrate the timeless power of the symphony while remaining accessible and relevant to contemporary audiences.

Our Journey

Our History

From humble beginnings to a cornerstone of Dayton's cultural landscape.

1990

The Founding — Founded by a group of passionate musicians with a vision to bring world-class orchestral music to the Miami Valley. The first concert draws 200 attendees to a small community center.

1995

Growing Pains — Ensemble expands to 40 musicians. First collaboration with Dayton schools launches educational programming that becomes a cornerstone of our mission.

2002

Professional Recognition — Performances at the Schuster Performing Arts Center begin. Dr. David Wilson joins as Music Director, bringing national prominence and expanded ambitions.

2010

Milestone: 20 Years — MVSO celebrates two decades of service with record attendance and expanded repertoire. Community support reaches all-time high.

2018

Youth Orchestra Launch — MVSO Youth Orchestra founded to develop young musicians and build the next generation of classical music advocates. Rapid growth exceeds expectations.

2024

Digital Innovation — Launch of streaming concerts and virtual performance series. MVSO reaches audiences far beyond Dayton, expanding our mission globally.

2026

The Future — MVSO continues to thrive with 70+ musicians, innovative programming, and unwavering commitment to making orchestral music accessible to all.

Are you ready to become part of something special?

Musician Auditions

The Miami Valley Symphony Orchestra welcomes accomplished musicians from all backgrounds. Whether you're a lifelong orchestral performer or returning to music after a hiatus, we invite you to audition.

Audition Requirements: Bring prepared orchestral excerpts from our repertoire, demonstrate strong ensemble skills, and show passion for collaborative music-making. We hold auditions annually in spring and by appointment throughout the season.

Next Generation

Youth Orchestra

Developing young musicians and fostering lifelong love of classical music.

MVSO Youth Orchestra

Founded in 2018, the MVSO Youth Orchestra provides young musicians ages 12-18 with professional training, performance opportunities, and mentorship from accomplished adult musicians. It's more than an ensemble — it's a community of passionate young artists dedicated to excellence.

Our youth musicians perform multiple concerts per season, participate in educational workshops, and collaborate with the main MVSO. Many of our youth orchestra alumni continue in music at the college level and beyond.
